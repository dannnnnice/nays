I chose to create this logo because I love chocolate, and I see it as versatile since it can be enjoyed in solid and liquid forms. The chocolate swirl at the top is the most important part because it shows the feeling of eating it, with a taste so good it feels like you are being swirled in flavor. Below that, the brand name “Velour Chocolat" is written in bright gold to show that it is a high-quality or "premium" brand, and the little drop of chocolate on the letter "V" makes it look fresh and mouth-watering. All in all, with its dark brown and gold tones, the logo does not just show a product—it tells a story of a luxurious treat that melts not only in the mouth, but lingers in the senses.
